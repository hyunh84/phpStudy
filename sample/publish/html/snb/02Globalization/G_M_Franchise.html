General Milestone toward Master Franchise Agreement (MFA)
1. Initial contact between franchisor and master franchisee candidate
2. Exchanging basic company introduction materials
3. Franchisor to present its offer package to MF candidate (incl. terms and conditions for MFA, MFA draft, and materials needed for feasibility study)
4. Candidate to review the offer and perform local market survey, P&L /ROI estimation, and etc.
5. Both parties to negotiate all the clauses of drafted MFA (by adding, deleting, and revising each wording – mostly with help from legal and accounting specialists)
6. Having face to face meeting at each other’s head office (not mandatory, but recommendable)
7. When all the terms and conditions are agreed and both parties are ready, It is time for the MFA to be signed and franchisor would deliver its all the know-how to the master franchise
